Pokémon Origins: Diamond-Pearl

What is Pokémon Origins: Diamond-Pearl?

Pokémon Origins: Diamond-Pearl is a fan-made anime series and is not approved by Game Freak or The Pokémon Company. Production on the trailer has already begun. The voices featured in the trailer have been cast and mostly recorded. The storyboard is nearly complete, and the animation phase will begin soon. Once more information is available, we’ll announce it on our YouTube channel.

How long will the series be?

The anime will have one season with 12 episodes. Each episode will be around 24 minutes long, as is standard for anime today. Each episode will likely take a year to produce, but with a staggered production system, we can release an episode every 6 months.

How can I help?

Once the trailer is released, auditions will be open for voice actors, designers, and animators to join the project. We need all the help we can get, so we’re hoping for many applicants.
