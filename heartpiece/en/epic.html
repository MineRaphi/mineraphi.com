Epic: The Musical - German Edition

What is Epic: The Musical

Epic: The Musical, written and composed by Jorge (Jay) Rivera-Herrans, aims to adapt the events of Homer's Odyssey into a musical format. The story begins in Troy, just before the Trojan Horse attack, and ends in Ithaca, Greece, the home island of the main character, Odysseus. In the original sung by Jorge himself, the German version features Mike Hinterlechner.

How can you audition?

Like with the original, this principal still applies:

Anyone can audition. - Jorge (Jay) Rivera-Herrans

Anyone can audition, no matter where in the world you live, whether you’re a native German speaker or still learning. Gender doesn’t matter at all; the only thing that counts is that your voice fits the role.
The most important things are that you sing confidently in German and are motivated to play your role.

How's the progress?

So far, 18 of the 35 already released songs have been translated, and demos for 2 of them have already been recorded and mixed. I hope to have the cast for the first 2 to 3 sagas selected by fall 2025, with the goal of releasing the Troy Saga by early 2026.
