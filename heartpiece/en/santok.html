The Legend of Santok: Mask of Power

What's this project about?

The Legend of Santok: Mask of Power is a fan film and still very early in the concept/script phase. The movie is about Noro, a 17 year old student from Tokyo who grew up in Shizuoka. Suddenly he is transported to the kingdom of Santok by a mysterious portal. There he learns that he is destined to to save the kingdom and defeat Tatsumi, the dark magician.

When will the film be released?

This question is pretty hard to answer at this point in time because we don't know yet how many people will work on this project and how big the team will grow. We expect the screenplay to be done mid to end of 2025. So a release date of 2030 could be expected. But we'll try to release the first trailer in the summer of 2026.
