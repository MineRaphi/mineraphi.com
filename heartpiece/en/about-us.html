About Us

Who even are we?

Founded in december of 2023 by Mike Hinterlechner, Heartpiece Anime/Synchron is an independant animation and dubbing studio based in Austria. Our goal is to bring heartwrenching stories to life with first class animation and great voice acting.

Here at Heartpiece we offer:

  • Anime-Production: From concept up until the finished product we'll do every part in anime production. Our small but powerfull team of animators and authors will work hard to bring visually stunning anime series and movies to life.
  • Dubbing: Our voice actors offer authentic and dynamic voice acting for anime. We will do our best to get the original nature emotions of every character accross, to deliver a seemless viewing experience.
  • Song Translation: Since the beginning of Epic the Musical: German Edition we have closely involved ourselves with musical song translation and are hoping to translate many more musicals into many languages.
