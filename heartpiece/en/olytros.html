Olytros: The Musical

What is Olytros: The Musical?

Olytros: The Musical is a brand new project which we started not that long ago, that's why only the rough story stands. When writing the songs, dialogue and scenes I'll incorporate inspirations from the Odyssey, the Uncharted games, Shounen Anime and other video games. When writing the songs we take inspiration from Alan Menken (Aladdin, Herkules, Sister Act), Jorge (Jay) Rivera-Herrans (Epic: The Musical, My Heart Says Go), Hans Zimmer (The Lion King, Pirates of the Caribbian, The Dark Knight), John Williams (Star Wars, Harry Potter, Jurassic Park) and John Powell (How to train your dragon, Kung Fu Panda).

What's the story like so far?

The Musical tells the story of the mexican american Leonardo Costas, nickname Leo, who wants to find the legendary, ancient city of Olytros and his lost father (Ju치n Costas). Together with his two friends Dave Jones and Jane Evans he starts in his hometown, Long Beach (Mississippi, USA). But on the way there they get into a storm and strand in Ivory Coast and meet Konan who had heard of the legends of Olytros himself and joins the group, in hopes that they would get to their goal easier. When they finally find their way into the mediteranian sea they loose orientation and look for the city in a cave they found. But this cave is located in Spain which the team doesn't notice. So they go inside and get trapped.
To know what happens next you'll have to wait until the musical is finished.
