Olytros: The Musical

Olytros: The Musical tells the story of the mexican american Leonardo Costas, who wants to find his lost father and goes through storms, strands in foreign lands and traps himself. On this jurney his two friends Dave Jones and Jane Evans are by his side. Leo's dad has often told him about the legends surrounding the ancient greek city of Olytros and got lost on his search to find it.

Epic: The Musical - German Edition

Epic: The Musical tells the story of the greek hero Odysseus and his jurney home after the trojan war. The original by Jorge (Jay) Rivera-Herrans hasn't been finished jet either, but about every 2 months a new album of the musical drops (such an album is called a Saga in the case of Epic). Act 1, which is made up of 20 songs in total, has been fully released. From act 2, which also is made up of 20 songs, 3 Sagas (15 Songs) have been released.

Pokémon Origins: Diamond-Pearl

Pokémon Origins: Diamond-Pearl is a pokémon fanseries which adapts the story of the fourth generation of the games. Similar to how Pokémon: Origins adapts the games of the first generation. It tells the story of Lucas, a 14 year old boy from Twinleaf Town who wants to become champ of the Sinnoh region. The series will have 12 episodes, each spanning 24 minutes. The first episode is now in production.
